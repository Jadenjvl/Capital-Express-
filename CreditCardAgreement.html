1. INTRODUCTION

This Credit Card Agreement (the "Agreement") outlines the terms and conditions for the use of your Capital Express credit card account. By using or activating your Capital Express credit card (the "Card"), you agree to comply with these terms.

2. CARD TYPES

You may apply for one of the following card types:

  • Capital Express First Class Travel (Preset Credit Limit)
  • Capital Express Platinum Card (No Preset Spending Limit)
  • Capital Express Gold Card (No Preset Spending Limit)
  • Capital Express Silver Card (No Preset Spending Limit)

Each card offers distinct rewards and benefits. Detailed benefits will be provided in the materials accompanying your card.

3. NO PRESET SPENDING LIMIT

For the Capital Express Platinum, Gold, and Silver Cards, there is no preset spending limit. This does not mean unlimited spending. Your ability to charge purchases will depend on various factors, including:

  • Your payment history
  • Your account activity
  • Your credit profile
  • Financial resources and other factors considered at the time of purchase

Your spending limit may vary and could be adjusted based on ongoing reviews of your account.

4. FEES AND INTEREST RATES

Annual Fees:

  • Capital Express First Class Travel: $139
  • Capital Express Platinum Card: $699
  • Capital Express Gold Card: $250
  • Capital Express Silver Card: Monthly Fee $10.99
  • The same for Business cards

Interest Rates:

  • Purchases: 20.99% per annum
  • Cash Advances: 24.99% per annum
  • Balance Transfers: 20.99% per annum

5. MINIMUM PAYMENT REQUIREMENTS

Your minimum payment each billing period will be:

  • $30 or 2% of your outstanding balance, whichever is greater.
  • Any past due amounts, plus any amount that exceeds your flexible spending amount (if applicable), must be paid in full immediately.

6. LATE PAYMENT FEE

If we do not receive your minimum payment by the due date, we may charge a late payment fee of $29.

7. CASH ADVANCES

Cash advances are subject to a higher interest rate (22.99%) and additional fees, including a cash advance fee of 5% of the amount advanced, with a minimum of $10.

8. LIABILITY FOR UNAUTHORIZED USE

If your card is lost or stolen, you must notify us immediately. You will not be held liable for unauthorized charges made after you report your card as lost or stolen. If unauthorized charges are made before you report the card, your liability will not exceed $250,000.

9. ACCOUNT CLOSURE

You may close your account at any time by notifying us. Any remaining balance must still be paid in full. We may also close your account or lower your spending ability at our discretion for reasons such as:

  • Non-payment or late payment
  • Fraud or misuse
  • Violation of this Agreement

10. CHANGES TO TERMS

We may change the terms of this Agreement, including interest rates and fees, with prior notice to you as required by law. You may reject any changes by closing your account.
